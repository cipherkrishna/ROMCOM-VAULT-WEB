1. Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for Rom-Com Vault Group Chat ("we," "us," or "our"). This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you join our group chat or interact with our website. By joining the Rom-Com Vault GC, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not join or use our services.

This privacy policy applies to all users of the Rom-Com Vault Group Chat, including moderators, admins, and members. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe, fun environment for discussing rom-com anime.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you submit the joining form. This may include:

  • Personal Information: Name/Nickname, Instagram Handle (optional), Favorite Rom-Com Anime, and Favorite Ship (optional).
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the group chat, such as messages, polls, and activities.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, and device details for security and analytics.

We do not collect sensitive information like payment details, as our services are free. All data is collected solely for group management and community building.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Approve and manage group chat memberships.
  • Facilitate discussions, polls, and events within the GC.
  • Improve our services, such as recommending anime or enhancing user experience.
  • Communicate updates, rules, or important announcements.
  • Ensure compliance with our rules and maintain a safe community.

We do not use your data for marketing or sell it to third parties. All activities are focused on creating a positive rom-com fan space.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: If you explicitly agree to share details, such as in public discussions.
  • Legal Requirements: To comply with laws, regulations, or legal processes.
  • Moderation and Safety: With admins or moderators to enforce rules or address violations.
  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help operate the GC (e.g., hosting platforms), bound by confidentiality.

Your Instagram handle or ship preferences may be visible in group interactions, but we encourage privacy settings for personal comfort.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If a data breach occurs, we will notify affected users promptly and take corrective actions. Your participation in the GC is at your own risk, and we recommend not sharing sensitive personal details.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy. For example:

  • Joining form data is kept for membership management and may be deleted upon request.
  • Chat logs and interactions are retained for moderation but anonymized after a period.
  • If you leave the GC, your data may be archived for legal or administrative reasons.

You can request deletion of your data by contacting us at privacy@romcomvault.com.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request removal of your data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format.
  • Objection: Opt out of certain uses, like non-essential communications.

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@romcomvault.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies to enhance your experience, such as remembering your theme preference or analyzing site traffic. Cookies are small files stored on your device. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

We do not use tracking for advertising. Any analytics are aggregated and anonymized to improve the site.

9. Third-Party Links

The GC may contain links to external sites, such as Instagram or anime databases. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. Please review their policies before interacting.

For example, if you share your Instagram handle, it links to your public profile, and we have no control over that platform's data handling.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it immediately. Parents or guardians can contact us to review or delete their child's data.

11. International Data Transfers

As a global community, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure adequate protections, such as standard contractual clauses, for international transfers.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Modified" date. We will notify members of significant changes via the GC. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
